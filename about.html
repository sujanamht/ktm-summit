Our Story

Kathmandu Summit Adventure

Kathmandu Summit Adventure Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a single, passionate vision: to share the extraordinary beauty, culture, and spirit of Nepal with travellers from around the world. Based in the heart of Kathmandu, we have grown from a small team of dedicated mountain enthusiasts into a fully licensed and certified adventure travel company trusted by hundreds of explorers every year.

We are registered with the Company Registration Office of the Government of Nepal, licensed by the Department of Tourism and Nepal Tourism Board (NTB), and proud members of both the Trekking Agency's Association of Nepal (TAAN) and the Nepal Mountaineering Association (NMA). These affiliations reflect our deep commitment to operating at the highest standards of professionalism, safety, and ethical tourism.

Our offerings span the full spectrum of adventure and cultural travel — from classic Himalayan treks like the Everest Base Camp and Annapurna Circuit, to immersive cultural tours across Nepal, Bhutan, and Tibet, to life-changing volunteering and internship programs. Whatever your journey looks like, our experienced local guides and support team are here to make it seamless, safe, and deeply memorable.

At Kathmandu Summit Adventure, we believe travel should do more than tick destinations off a bucket list. It should connect you with the people, places, and stories that make the Himalayas one of the most remarkable regions on earth. That belief drives everything we do — and we look forward to being part of your adventure.
