About Us

Kathmandu Summit Adventure —
A Premier Adventure Company in Nepal

Kathmandu Summit Adventure is a licensed and certified trekking & tour agency based in Kathmandu, Nepal. Registered with the Department of Tourism, Nepal Tourism Board (NTB), TAAN, and NMA, we have been connecting travellers with the breathtaking landscapes, vibrant cultures, and warm communities of Nepal, Bhutan, and Tibet for years.

Whether you seek the thrill of a Himalayan trek, a cultural immersion, a wildlife safari, or a meaningful volunteering experience, our expert team is dedicated to crafting journeys that leave a lasting impression.
